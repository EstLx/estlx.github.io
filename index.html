About me

Who am I ?
I'm Estelle, a french young woman who grew up in the suburbs of Paris. I left my hometown to move to Lyon in 2014, where I found what would be my new home. I love to learn new things, like new languages or mastering news skills, like playing the piano. I also like cats, handcraft, video games and coffee.

My background
Freshly graduated from INSA Lyon, I am a computer science engineer based in Lyon. I started my studies in Paris at Paris Descartes University where I got a technology degree in Computer Science. Then I entered one of the best engineering school, INSA Lyon as a Computer Science student. There, I started to learn more about UX and UI design, and my interest in these topics never stopped growing. Now I am working as a web and mobile developper in Lyon, while I'm still learning and experimenting more about design and ergonomy on my own.
